I am currently a doctoral researcher at [IMT Atlantique - Brest](https://www.imt-atlantique.fr/fr) in the Micro-Ondes department. My research focus on the study of spin waves (magnons) for applications in neuromorphic computing and signal processing. 

Academia
======
I did my M.Sc. in Physics at the [Institute of Physics, UNAM](https://www.fisica.unam.mx/) in Mexico, under the supervision of [Dr. Yuri G. Rubo](https://www.ier.unam.mx/academicos/ygr/), graduating with honors. My research was devoted to the creation of a quantum theory for the analysis of a polaritonic condensate with Parity-Time symmetry which also allowed the study of the semiclassical limit. After that I had the opportunity to be a research assistant at [IFT, UAM](https://www.ift.uam-csic.es/) in Madrid, under the supervision of [Dr. Alejandro Bermudez Carballo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/alejandro-bermudez-4a180627/). This research investigated the symmetric and anisotropic surface codes, and the XZZX surface code under biased noise, in the context of quantum error correction for trapped-ion qubits.

I am now a doctoral researcher at [IMT Atlantique - Brest](https://www.imt-atlantique.fr/fr) in the Micro-Ondes department, under the supervision of [Dr. Vincent Vlaminck](https://labsticc.fr/en/directory/vlaminck-vincent). My research focuses on the study of a Magnon Interferometry which is funded by the [PEPR SPIN - SWING project](https://www.pepr-spin.fr/projet/swing/).

I am passionate about [teaching](https://jesan-velazquez-resendiz.github.io//teaching/). I have been a teaching assistant in three undergraduate courses for electromagnetism and a preparatory course for the Physics Graduate program at UNAM, Mexico. Sharing knowledge is one of my biggest dreams.
